Like Tinder,
but for movies.

What is it for?

Trying to pick a movie to watch with someone else can be a nightmare. Cinematch lets you individually swipe through movies and decide what you want to watch, and then notifies you when everyone in your group has liked the same movie.

How does it work?

Create a session with the kind of movies you all want to watch and pick a unique (but memorable!) session name. You can narrow the movies down based on release years, genre, and any streaming services you have.

You also need to specify how many likes a movie needs before you get a match. For example, if three of you are trying to pick something, set this to three.

Once you’ve created a session, share the name with everyone else and start swiping through movies. Once a movie gets enough likes you'll all be notified.
